Admissions

Welcome to Admissions

The College of Home Economics, University of Peshawar, welcomes female students to apply for its undergraduate, graduate, and postgraduate programs. Our admissions process is designed to select students with a passion for learning and a commitment to excellence in Home Economics.

Eligibility Criteria

  • Undergraduate Programs (BS in Home Economics): Candidates must have completed their intermediate education (F.A/F.Sc or equivalent) with a minimum of 45% marks.
  • Graduate Programs (MS in Home Economics): Applicants must have a bachelor's degree in Home Economics or a related field with a minimum of 2.5 CGPA or 50% marks.

Application Process

  1. Download the application form from the official website or collect it from the college admission office.
  2. Fill out the application form with accurate details and attach all required documents.
  3. Submit the completed form to the admission office before the deadline.
  4. Pay the application processing fee at the designated bank branch.
  5. Shortlisted candidates will be called for an interview or written test (if applicable).

Required Documents

  • Attested copies of academic certificates and mark sheets
  • Two recent passport-sized photographs
  • Copy of CNIC/Form-B
  • Character certificate from the last attended institution
  • Bank receipt of the application processing fee

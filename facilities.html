Facilities

Our College Facilities

The college offers a wide range of facilities to provide students with an excellent academic and extracurricular environment. These facilities include:

  • Library: A well-stocked library with books, journals, and digital resources to support learning and research.
  • Computer Labs: State-of-the-art computer labs equipped with high-speed internet and modern software.
  • Science Laboratories: Fully equipped labs for physics, chemistry, and biology practicals.
  • Sports Facilities: Grounds and equipment for cricket, football, volleyball, and indoor games.
  • Cafeteria: A hygienic and spacious cafeteria offering a variety of meals and snacks.
  • Medical Center: On-campus medical facilities with first aid and emergency care available.
  • Auditorium: A large auditorium for seminars, workshops, and cultural events.
  • Transportation: College buses for easy commuting from various parts of the city.
  • Hostel Facilities: Comfortable and secure hostels for outstation students.

We continuously strive to enhance our facilities to ensure the best possible environment for our students' academic and personal growth.

Student Societies

The societies are run by a President and a Vice-President, who are selected by a team of senior faculty members and the Proctorial board of the college. The students' association also includes General Secretaries, Class Representatives, selected from each class of the college. Annual installation ceremony of the Students' association conducted every year where selected members are awarded badges to the new elected cabinet members. The same function is celebrated inviting chief guest from well-repute institution to award and encourage the young females for political and social activities.

Each society prepares various competitions, exhibitions, and physical activities within the college in order to prepare them to compete on national and international levels. Highlights of few activities arranged by various societies are:

  • Milaad Sharif - Organized by Religious society
  • First Aid Training - Organized by Social welfare society
  • Flower/Jewelry Competition - Organized by Creative art society
  • Friendly Cricket Match - Organized by Sports society

Activities

Extracurricular Activities

Our college encourages students to participate in a variety of extracurricular activities to enhance their skills and engage in healthy competition. Here are some of the key activities organized annually:

  • Sports Events: Friendly matches and annual sports day including cricket, football, and athletics.
  • Cultural Programs: Events to celebrate and showcase diverse traditions and cultures.
  • Workshops: Skill-based workshops and training sessions to enhance learning beyond the classroom.
  • Debate Competitions: Inter-departmental and inter-collegiate debate contests to foster critical thinking and public speaking skills.
  • Art Exhibitions: Showcasing student talent in painting, sculpture, and crafts.
  • Science Fairs: Opportunities for students to present innovative projects and ideas.

Annual Celebrations

The college hosts an annual day event where students are awarded for their academic and extracurricular achievements. Highlights include:

  • Prize distribution ceremony
  • Cultural performances
  • Guest lectures by notable alumni or experts

Student-Led Initiatives

Students are actively involved in organizing and leading various initiatives, such as:

  • Community service programs
  • Environmental awareness campaigns
  • Peer mentoring and tutoring sessions

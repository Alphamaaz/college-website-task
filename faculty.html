Our Faculty

Meet Our Dedicated Faculty

The College of Home Economics, University of Peshawar, is proud to have a highly qualified and experienced faculty that is committed to providing quality education and fostering research. Our faculty members are dedicated professionals who are passionate about teaching and mentoring students to achieve academic excellence and career success.

Faculty Members

  • Dr. Shehnaz Khattak - Principal
    Ph.D. in Home Economics
    With extensive experience in teaching and research, Dr. Khattak leads the college with vision and dedication.

  • Dr. Ayesha Khan - Head of Human Development & Family Studies
    Ph.D. in Psychology
    Dr. Khan specializes in child development, family dynamics, and counseling.

  • Ms. Fatima Bibi - Senior Lecturer in Textile & Clothing
    M.S. in Textile Design
    An expert in fabric design and fashion, Ms. Bibi inspires creativity in her students.

  • Dr. Saima Akbar - Head of Food & Nutrition
    Ph.D. in Nutrition
    Dr. Akbar focuses on community health and nutritional research.

  • Ms. Hina Javed - Lecturer in Interior Design
    M.Sc. in Interior Architecture
    Ms. Javed is passionate about designing functional and aesthetic spaces.

  • Dr. Maria Shah - Head of Management Studies
    Ph.D. in Business Administration
    Dr. Shah teaches entrepreneurship, organizational management, and leadership.

Commitment to Excellence

Our faculty members are not only educators but also mentors, researchers, and leaders in their respective fields. They work tirelessly to create a supportive and innovative learning environment that encourages students to excel academically and professionally.

Programs Offered

The College of Home Economics at the University of Peshawar offers a variety of programs designed to equip female students with the necessary knowledge and skills to meet the challenges of a modern society. The programs combine elements of science and art, providing students with professional expertise and opening doors to diverse career opportunities. Below is an overview of the programs offered:

Undergraduate Programs

  • Bachelor of Science (BS) in Home Economics: A 4-year undergraduate program with five specialized disciplines, aimed at preparing students for professional roles in interior design, textile design, small business management, teaching, and research.

Graduate Programs

  • Master of Science (MS) in Home Economics: A postgraduate program designed for students who wish to deepen their knowledge and pursue advanced studies in Home Economics.

Specialized Disciplines

The college offers programs in the following specialized fields:

  • Interior Design: Equips students with the skills to design and manage interior spaces creatively and efficiently.
  • Textile Design: Focuses on the creation of textiles with innovative patterns and designs, preparing students for careers in the textile industry.
  • Human Development & Family Studies: Teaches students about family dynamics, child development, and counseling skills to support families in diverse settings.
  • Management Studies: Provides skills in business management, entrepreneurship, and leadership for those aspiring to manage organizations or start their ventures.
  • Food & Nutrition: Focuses on the science of nutrition, food preparation, and dietetics to promote healthy lifestyles and careers in nutrition-related fields.

Future Plans

The college is actively working on introducing new programs and enhancing existing ones. Planned initiatives include:

  • Launching a sixth discipline of Home Economics to further expand career opportunities for students.
  • Introducing a distance learning program for students who cannot attend regular classes.
  • Offering diploma and certificate courses in specialized areas of Home Economics.

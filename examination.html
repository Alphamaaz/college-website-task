Short Title, Commencement and Application:

  1. These regulations shall be called the “University of Peshawar Semester Regulations 2010”.
  2. These regulations shall come into force with effect from academic session 2010-2011.
  3. These regulations shall be applicable to all the degree programs offered by the University of Peshawar or its constituent or affiliated institutions under semester system.

Definitions:

In these regulations, unless the context otherwise requires, the following expressions shall have the meanings hereby respectively assigned to them:

  • “Head” means Chairperson of a Department, or Principal of a College, or Director of an Institute/Academy/Centre.
  • “Institution” means a constituent or affiliated department / college / institute / centre / academy of the university.
  • “Dean” means Dean of a Faculty of University of Peshawar.
  • “Departmental Coordinator of Semester System” means a faculty member of the department/college/institute/centre/academy who has been assigned the duty of coordinating semester system in a department by the Head of respective department.
  • “Departmental Semester Committee” means a committee constituted under these regulations.

Duration of Semester:

  • Each semester shall be of 18 weeks duration; out of which 16 weeks shall be reserved for teaching, and 2 weeks for examinations.
  • A ‘Summer Semester’ shall be of 10 weeks duration, i.e., 8 weeks for teaching, and 2 weeks for examination. However, the contact hours during the Summer Semester will be enhanced to ensure that the course is completely taught / covered.
  • There shall be a ‘teaching break’, to be called ‘Semester Break’, of 2 weeks after every semester; this break will be used for preparation/submission of results and admission/registration processes for next semester.
  • If teaching in the whole of the university is suspended due to some exigency, the period of the semester shall be either extended to the extent of the duration lost due to this or by offering additional teaching/contact hours per week during the remaining part of the semester.
  • There will be two terms/tests in One Semester i.e., Mid Term Test (8 weeks teaching + 9th week for examination) and Final Term Test (8 weeks of teaching + 18th week for examination).
  • A semester will be considered as valid if 80% lectures are delivered.
  • There will be no classes on Saturdays and Sundays.

Academic Year/Session:

  • Fall Semester: Starting from First Monday of September to second week of January.
  • Spring Semester: Starting from First Monday of February to the second week of June.
  • Summer Semester: During summer vacations to be used for offering minor/related or special courses, OR in special circumstances.

Admissions:

  • Admissions for 4-years Undergraduate Degree Programme (BS) and the ongoing MA/M.Sc shall be made for Fall Semester only.
  • Admission to BS 4-Year, Master 2-Year, MPhil & MS will be announced within one week of the declaration of Intermediate, Bachelor, and Master Examinations respectively and the process shall be completed within one month prior to commencement of semester.
  • If the number of students admitted in a programme is less than 15, the admission will be considered as cancelled.
